About the foundation

A nonprofit partner for economic opportunity and community well-being.

We support projects, partnerships, and programs that help the Chino Valley community grow with intention, inclusion, and long-term value.

Our vision

Prosperity that works for residents, businesses, and future leaders.

We envision a Chino Valley where economic opportunities are abundant and accessible, where innovation is encouraged, and where businesses and community life support one another.

Core values

What guides our work.

Community empowerment

Initiatives that help residents, stakeholders, and local organizations take action.

Economic innovation

Projects that encourage sustainable business growth and workforce readiness.

Collaboration

Partnerships with people, businesses, schools, and civic organizations.

Transparency

Decisions made with integrity and a clear focus on community benefit.

Inclusivity

Opportunities designed to be accessible to people from every background.

Sustainability

Projects that serve present needs while protecting future community value.

Impact

Through partnerships, projects, and programs, the Foundation supports local businesses, community development, education, workforce readiness, and civic engagement.
